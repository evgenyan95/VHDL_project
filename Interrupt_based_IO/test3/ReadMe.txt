Input:  KEY3, KEY2, KEY1
Output: PORT_LEDG[7-0], PORT_LEDR[7-0]
RESET:  Pushbutton KEY0
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
On reset - counting up by 1 in frequency of 24MHz/2^24
On KEY1 pushing - counting up by 1 in frequency of 24MHz/2^22
On KEY2 pushing - counting up by 1 in frequency of 24MHz/2^21
On KEY3 pushing - counting up by 1 in frequency of 24MHz/2^20
